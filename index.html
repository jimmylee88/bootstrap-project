Frequently Asked Questions

Manchester Wine Tours are for anyone who likes wine whether you're a passionate enthusiast or someone who just likes a glass or two. They're not for kids though, you must be 18+ to ride.

A Manchester Wine Tour is £75 per person and includes all drinks, snacks, fully curated itinerary and a friendly, wine expert tour guide.

You will taste at least six small glasses of really good wine equating to roughly 2/3 of a bottle per person in total.

Many of our venues offer wine you can buy to take home. You are also welcome to stay at the last venue and buy more wine.

This is not a food tour, there are already great ones in Manchester. But we don't like to drink wine on its own so there is a small but elite snack at every venue. Please let us know if you have any dietary requirements. We cater to vegans, gluten free and special diets.

Only if it’s a registered guide dog, some venues are not dog friendly.

We have hand selected our favourite places for wine in Manchester but the list is still growing. If you run a city centre wine-focused bar, shop or restaurant and want us to consider calling at your place, drop us an email.
